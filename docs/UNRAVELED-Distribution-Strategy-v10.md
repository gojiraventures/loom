## 1. Owned Channels — The Content Ecosystem

Every channel serves a different purpose in the funnel. The site is the research hub. Social is the discovery layer. The app is the retention engine.

```
Discovery (Social) → Engagement (Site) → Depth (Membership) → Habit (App)

Instagram / TikTok / X ──→ unraveled.ai ──→ Membership ──→ App
   "Wait, WHAT?"              Deep dives           Process        Daily
   60-sec facts               Full research         access        micro-learning
   Shareable cards             Scripture comps       Research log  Bite-size chapters
   Map animations              People dossiers       Live sessions Notifications
```

### Channel Strategy

| Channel | Content Type | Posting Frequency | Purpose |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Instagram** | Designed fact cards, carousel breakdowns, scripture comparisons as visual slides, short Reels | 5-7x/week | Visual discovery. The "Wait, WHAT?" factory. Highly shareable static cards + carousels. |
| **TikTok** | 60-90 second "jaw drop" facts, "things your Bible actually says" series, map animations, "this man decided you wouldn't know..." dossier reveals | 5-7x/week | Viral discovery. Younger audience. Algorithm favors niche educational content. |
| **X (Twitter)** | Threads breaking down convergence points, single-fact tweets with source citations, researcher threads, community engagement | Daily | Discourse. The place for the detailed arguments, the Advocate/Skeptic debates, and engagement with researchers. |
| **YouTube** | Long-form deep dives (15-30 min), "The Unraveled" series, artifact walkthroughs, scripture read-throughs in original languages, researcher interviews | 2-4x/month | Depth. The bridge between social discovery and the full site. Monetizable through YouTube Partner Program. |
| **YouTube Shorts** | Same as TikTok content, cross-posted | 5-7x/week | Discovery + YouTube algorithm fuel for the long-form content. |
| **Substack** | Weekly newsletter: "This week in Unraveled" — new findings, new connections, behind-the-research stories | Weekly | Email list building. Direct relationship with audience. Not dependent on algorithm. |
| **Podcast** | "The Unraveled Podcast" — deep conversations about each topic, interviews with researchers, Advocate vs Skeptic debates | 2x/month | Long-form audio. Podcast audiences are the most loyal and highest-converting to membership. |

### Content Format Templates

**Instagram Carousel — "5 Things You Didn't Know About [Topic]"**
```
Slide 1: Bold statement + "Swipe to have your mind changed"
Slide 2: Jaw-drop fact #1 with source
Slide 3: Jaw-drop fact #2 with source  
Slide 4: Jaw-drop fact #3 with visual

**X Thread — Unraveled Breakdown**
```

```

**YouTube Long-Form — "The [Topic] Unraveled"**
```

## 2. Content Licensing — The Influencer Engine

### The Opportunity

There are thousands of creators who make content about ancient mysteries, biblical topics, mythology, and alternative history. Most of them have audiences but terrible sourcing. We have the sourcing but need the audience. Perfect trade.

### Licensing Tiers

| Tier | Who It's For | What They Get | Cost | Requirements |
|------|-------------|---------------|------|-------------|
| **Free / Attribution** | Anyone | Use any single fact, stat, or quote from our public content with visible attribution ("Source: Unraveled") and a link back to the specific page. | Free | Must include "Source: Unraveled — unraveled.ai" visibly in content. Must link to the specific source page. |
| **Creator License** | YouTubers, podcasters, TikTokers, bloggers with 1K-100K audience | Use up to 5 facts/graphics per piece. Access to our designed shareable assets (cards, maps, graphics). "Powered by Unraveled" badge. | $10-25/month | Attribution required. Link back required. Cannot claim our research as their own original work. |
| **Pro License** | Larger creators, media companies, documentary producers, authors | Unlimited use of our structured data, graphics, and research. White-label scripture comparisons and convergence scores. Direct research support for specific projects. | $100-500/month or per-project | Attribution required. Negotiable for large projects. |
| **Embed License** | Anyone who wants to embed our interactive elements | Embed our maps, timelines, scripture comparisons, and unraveled cards directly into their site/app. Our branding stays on the embed. | Free (with branding) or $50/month (white-label) | Embeds must load from our servers (we control the content and get the analytics). |

### How Embeds Work (The Traffic Engine)

This is the highest-leverage play. We build embeddable widgets that other sites can drop into their content:

```html
<!-- Anyone can embed this on their site -->
<iframe src="https://unraveled.ai/embed/flood-timeline" 
        width="100%" height="600" frameborder="0">
</iframe>
```

**What we embed:**
- The narrative spread map (with time slider)
- The scripture side-by-side comparisons
- Individual unraveled cards with expand functionality
- The irrefutable facts carousel
- Topic-specific timelines

**Why this is powerful:**
- The embed lives on THEIR site but loads from OUR servers
- Every view counts as OUR traffic and OUR engagement
- The embed has our branding and a "View full research" link
- It's free for them (they get great content for their audience)
- It's free customer acquisition for us (their audience discovers us)
- AI crawlers see our content on multiple domains (boosts GEO authority)

**The YouTube-of-research-embeds model:** Just like YouTube embeds made YouTube the default video platform by distributing across every website, our research embeds could make Unraveled the default citation source for this content category.

### Influencer Outreach Strategy

| Influencer Type | How We Approach | What We Offer |
|----------------|----------------|---------------|
| **Academic YouTubers** (Religion for Breakfast, UsefulCharts, etc.) | Offer structured research support for their videos. "We noticed your video on X — here's our cross-cultural comparison with 8 primary sources you might find useful." | Free research access + designed graphics they can use with attribution |
| **Alternative History creators** (careful vetting) | Only approach creators who cite sources. Offer to strengthen their sourcing. "Your video on Göbekli Tepe was interesting — we have the excavation reports if you want to cite primary sources instead of secondary ones." | Creator License + source verification help |
| **Podcast hosts** | Offer ourselves as a research backbone / recurring segment. "The Unraveled Report — what our AI research agents found this month." | Recurring segment deal + cross-promotion |
| **Religious content creators** | Offer the scripture comparisons and biblically accurate angel content. This audience is massive and hungry for quality content. | Free use with attribution. The "Things Your Bible Actually Says" content practically markets itself. |

### The Attribution Flywheel

```
We create structured research
  → Influencer uses it with attribution + link
    → Their audience clicks through to us
      → Some become members
        → Membership funds more research
          → More research creates more citable content
            → More influencers use it
              → More attribution links
                → More GEO authority
                  → AI engines cite us more
```

Every link back to us from an influencer's content is both a traffic driver AND a GEO signal. The more sites that cite us, the more AI engines trust us as an authority, the more they cite us in AI-generated responses.

## 3. The Micro-Learning App — "Unraveled Daily"

### The Vision

An app that turns idle time into discovery. Bathroom break? Learn one jaw-drop fact. Lunch break? Read one chapter of the Nephilim deep dive. Commute? Listen to one scripture comparison.

This is not the full research site shrunk to mobile. It's a purpose-built **micro-learning experience** designed for daily engagement in 2-5 minute sessions.

### Core App Features

**Daily Discovery (Home Screen)**
Every day, the app serves you one new piece of content from the knowledge base:
- A jaw-drop fact you haven't seen
- A scripture comparison
- A person dossier reveal
- A unraveled connection
- An irrefutable fact with source

Swipe to save. Swipe to share. Tap to go deeper.

**Micro-Chapters**
Each major topic is broken into 15-25 bite-sized chapters that can be read in 2-3 minutes each. Progress tracking. Bookmark where you left off.

Example — "The Great Flood" in micro-chapters:
```
Chapter 1:  "More Than Noah" — the 268-narrative overview (2 min read)
Chapter 2:  "The Oldest Story" — the Eridu Genesis, 4000 BCE (2 min)
Chapter 3:  "The Tablet That Changed Everything" — Gilgamesh XI (3 min)
Chapter 4:  "The Bird Test" — comparing the bird-release sequences (2 min)
Chapter 5:  "The Fish God's Warning" — the Hindu Matsya account (2 min)
Chapter 6:  "The Failed Creation" — the Maya Popol Vuh flood (2 min)
Chapter 7:  "The Engineering Solution" — China's Gun-Yu (2 min)
Chapter 8:  "Blood and Ice" — the Norse flood of Ymir (2 min)
Chapter 9:  "The Physical Evidence" — Black Sea, Shuruppak, Yellow River (3 min)
Chapter 10: "The Map" — watching the stories spread across continents (2 min)
Chapter 11: "What the Advocate Says" — the case for unraveled (3 min)
Chapter 12: "What the Skeptic Says" — the case for coincidence (3 min)
Chapter 13: "The Open Questions" — what nobody can explain (2 min)
Chapter 14: "The Sources" — every citation, every link (1 min)
Chapter 15: "What Do You Think?" — save your verdict, see community results (1 min)
```

30 minutes total. Absorbed in 15 sessions over 2 weeks. By the end, the user knows more about cross-cultural flood narratives than 99% of people including most academics — and they got there in bathroom breaks.

**Streak & Progress**
- Daily streak counter (gentle, not manipulative — "You've explored for 7 days straight")
- Topic completion tracking (you've read 8/15 chapters of the Flood)
- "Knowledge Map" showing which topics you've explored and which are untouched
- Total facts discovered counter

**Quick Share**
Every micro-chapter and daily fact has a one-tap share button that generates a designed card optimized for Instagram Stories, X, and iMessage. The card always includes: the fact, the source, and the Unraveled URL.

**Offline Mode**
Chapters are lightweight text. Cache the current topic for offline reading. Perfect for planes, subways, or anywhere without signal.

**Audio Mode**
Every micro-chapter can be listened to as audio (text-to-speech or recorded narration). The scripture comparisons are particularly powerful in audio — hearing Genesis in Hebrew followed by Gilgamesh in reconstructed Akkadian.

### App Monetization

The app is **free** with the core experience:
- Daily discovery (1 fact/day)
- First 3 chapters of each topic
- Basic sharing

**Premium unlock ($4.99/month or included with site membership):**
- Unlimited daily discoveries
- All chapters for all topics
- Audio mode
- Offline downloads
- Ad-free (there are no ads anyway — this is just clarity)
- Early access to new topics before they hit the public site

### App Tech Stack

| Layer | Technology |
|-------|-----------|
| **Framework** | React Native (Expo) — single codebase for iOS + Android |
| **Backend** | Same Supabase instance as the main site |

## 4. Content Production Pipeline

### Weekly Output Targets

| Content Type | Quantity | Platform | Time Investment |
|-------------|----------|----------|----------------|
| Instagram fact cards | 5 | Instagram | 3 hours/week |
| TikTok/Reels | 4 | TikTok, IG Reels, YT Shorts | 4 hours/week |
| X threads | 2 | X/Twitter | 2 hours/week |
| Newsletter | 1 | Substack | 2 hours/week |
| YouTube long-form | 1 | YouTube | 8 hours/week |
| Podcast episode | 1 (biweekly) | Spotify, Apple, YouTube | 4 hours/biweekly |
| Site content update | 1 new section or topic expansion | unraveled.ai | 10 hours/week |
| App micro-chapters | 3-5 new chapters | App | 3 hours/week |
| **Total** | | | **~34 hours/week** |

### AI-Assisted Production

Much of this can be AI-accelerated:
- **Fact cards:** AI extracts the jaw-drop facts from existing research → human designs the card
- **Thread writing:** AI drafts the thread from the convergence point data → human edits for voice and accuracy
- **Video scripts:** AI generates script from the deep dive → human refines and records
- **Micro-chapters:** AI breaks the full research into chapter-length segments → human edits for flow and accuracy
- **Newsletter:** AI summarizes the week's research activity → human adds editorial voice

The research is the hard part (and can't be fully automated). The distribution is the part AI makes scalable.

### The Repurposing Matrix

One piece of deep research becomes 10+ pieces of content:

```
1 Deep Dive (e.g., "Biblically Accurate Angels")
  ├── 1 YouTube long-form (25 min)
  ├── 5 TikTok/Reels (60-sec jaw-drops)
  ├── 5 Instagram carousels (swipeable breakdowns)
  ├── 3 X threads (scripture comparisons, dossier reveals, convergence scores)
  ├── 1 Newsletter feature
  ├── 1 Podcast episode
  ├── 15-20 App micro-chapters
  ├── 10+ Shareable fact cards
  ├── 2-3 Embeddable widgets for other sites
  └── 1 Designed poster/print for merch
```

40+ pieces of content from a single research effort. That's the leverage.

## 5. Platform-Specific Monetization

| Platform | How It Monetizes | Our Take |
|----------|-----------------|----------|
| **YouTube** | Partner Program (ads on our videos, we don't control ad content but it's expected on YT) | Accept. YouTube ads are expected by the audience. The revenue funds production. Not the same as running ads on our research site. |
| **Instagram** | No direct monetization — drives traffic to site | Use for discovery and brand building only. |
| **TikTok** | Creator Fund + TikTok Shop (merch integration) | Creator Fund is minimal but free money. TikTok Shop could sell merch directly. |
| **X/Twitter** | Subscription revenue share (if eligible) | Minimal but accept if offered. |
| **Substack** | Paid subscriber option | Keep free. Use for email list building, not revenue. Revenue comes from site membership. |
| **Podcast** | Sponsorship reads (selective) | Only accept sponsors that align with the mission — book publishers, educational platforms, tools for researchers. Never supplements, VPNs, or mattresses. |
| **App** | Premium subscription ($4.99/month) | Core free, premium for power features. |

**The principle:** YouTube ads are fine because that's the platform's norm. Site ads are not fine because they undermine our credibility. The audience understands the difference.
